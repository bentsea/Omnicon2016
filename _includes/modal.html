Todd Haberkorn

Producer, Writer, Director, Voice Actor

About Todd

Todd Haberkorn has been on stage, on set, and behind a mic for many years. Not only is Todd an actor in LA, he works as a producer, writer and director as well. And he's even been an action figure or two. Haberkornmobile sold separately.

In the world of anime, Todd got his first major start in Suzuka as Yamato Akitski. From there, he went on to voice roles such as Jadeite from Sailor Moon, Natsu from Fairy Tail, Italy from Hetalia, Ling Yao from Full Metal Alchemist: Brotherhood, Cheren from Pokemon, Allen Walker from D. Gray Man, Hikaru Hitachin from Ouran High School Host Club, Death The Kid from Soul Eater, and many others. As far as video games go, you'd recognize his voice in the Call of Duty Series, Defiance, World of Warcraft, Street Fighter IV, Ghostbusters, Silent Hill: Book of Memories, Borderlands 2, and Rage to name a handful. His voice makes sure to get its marathon training in as an award winning narrator for audiobooks. Do a Google search and you'll find everything from thrillers and young adult to zombie sagas and children's books.

As far as on camera is concerned, when he isn't working on films, industrials, and television projects for other companies, Todd does work with his company Out of the Office Productions. This is one aspect of a long history of on camera work in narrative film, commercials, and shorts. When he isn't fighting digital monsters in video games or trying to save the day on film, Todd travels in the U.S. and internationally making appearances at gnarly pop culture conventions as a guest to meet super cool fans and sign autographs. He is represented by The Horne Agency, Jeff Zannini Management, Johnson Talent Agency, and William Morris Endeavor.

Ruth Connell

Actress

About Me

Ruth Connell is currently appearing as the new recurring character "Rowena" in Supernatural on the CW Network. This is Ruth’s US TV debut. Since moving to LA, Ruth has played lead roles in the award winning Indi film “Folklore” playing a savagely reluctant Vampire and as a powerful Sorceress in the short “Sapphire Strange” which she helped produce. She is Disney Pixar’s official voice match for Princess Merida in conjunction with the Oscar winning movie “Brave”.

Born in Scotland, her first professional performance was on stage with Scottish Ballet whom she performed with in many Ballets including playing the young lead role of Clara in their “Nutcracker”. A former Scottish Ballet Champion, Ruth is a classically trained actor having gained a BA HONS from Rose Bruford College in London with ‘Critic’s Choice’ for her graduation showcase.

Her portrayal of Mrs Darling and Captain Hook in “Peter Pan; The Boy Who Hated Mothers” at Noah Wylie’s The Blank Theatre LA, saw Ruth nominated as "Best Leading Actress" in the BroadwayworldLA awards 2013.

She has played many principal roles on the main stages in the UK; from Shakespeare and Faust at The Royal Lyceum, to kitchen-sink drama at Hackney Empire. Her brogue can be heard on many campaigns for the Scottish Government as well as in commercials worldwide. Ruth also co-created The Avenue Theatre Company in Greenwich, London and produced their two sell out seasons there.

Her film debut was in “Soldiers Leap” with Billy Boyd and her last TV role in the UK was in a Sitcom for ITV with Denis Lawson where she played a Ukrainian cleaning lady who was really an ex-soviet gymnast and spy in disguise… in “Above Their Stations”.

Ellenor Fish

Cosplayer

About me

Ellenor Fish is a cosplayer from South Texas, but started her nerdy journey in the mountains of Colorado. With only dial-up internet and Toonami, it was a true task to be a part of the anime community, and cosplaying at a convention was only a dream. She began cosplaying just for fun at the age of 12, and always held anime, games, and cosplay in a special place in her heart. After moving to Corpus Christi, Texas she was close to giving up her dream of cosplaying at conventions when she made friends with other local cosplayers, and nothing was ever the same again.

Ellenor joined a cosplay group with her friends, and her passion for cosplay, performance, and entertainment has only grown stronger. Over the years, Ellenor has competed in cosplay contests (solo and with groups!) all over Texas, winning several judge’s awards, best skit, and best craftsmanship.

Also being the founder of her city’s local Lolita Community. She’s ran 2 fashion shows, multiple panels, and many large scale meet ups for her local lolita community. She is considered her communities go-to mentor for lolita fashion.

Over all, Ellenor considers herself a professional goofy-goober that loves Japanese fashion and cosplaying, and her journey has only just begun! Come say hello and join the party!”

Erica Mendez

Voice Actress

About Me

Erika Mendez is an American voice actress from Chicago, Illinois. Her first major voice role was the title character Pac-Man in the Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures video game. She then later voiced the starring character Aladdin in the Magi: The Labyrinth of Magic series and Ryuko Matoi in the English dub of Kill la Kill.
