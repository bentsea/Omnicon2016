McAllen
McAllen Convention center, 700 Convention Center Blvd

McAllen Texas is known for its palm trees, food, and every summer one of the best Pop Culture events in South Texas. Omnicon returns for its 9th year at the McAllen convention center. Spend August 5th and 6th shopping in our vendors room, competing in our Nexus Video game Tournaments, and seeing all of the great cosplays Texas has to offer. Meet up with your friends or make some new ones at this years Omnicon.

The Omnicon hotel is the La Quinta McAllen Convention Center. Omnicon attendees may take advantage of a special rate of $102.60(single)/$107.10 (Double) a night and enjoy free breakfast and a VIP event only available for hotel guests. To take advantage of this special rate and VIP event, call the La Quinta McAllen Convention Center and ask for the Omnicon rate:
